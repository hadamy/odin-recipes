Shawarma's Recipe

Description

Shawarma is a beloved Middle Eastern dish that has gained popularity worldwide for its delicious and aromatic flavors. It typically consists of thinly sliced cuts of marinated meat, such as lamb, chicken, beef, or sometimes a combination thereof. The meat is stacked in a cone-like shape on a vertical rotisserie, allowing it to cook slowly and evenly as it rotates.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • marinade
  • flatbread
  • vegetables
  • sauces and condiments

steps

  1. Prepare the Marinade
  2. Marinate the meat
  3. Cook the meat
  4. Prepare the Accompaniments
  5. Assemble the Shawarma
  6. Wrap and Serve
