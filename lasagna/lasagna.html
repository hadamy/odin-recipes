lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its layers of pasta, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel (white sauce), cheese, and often a savory filling such as ground meat or vegetables

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Béchamel Sauce
  • Meat Filling
  • Cheese

steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
  2. Make the Béchamel Sauce
  3. Prepare the Lasagna Sheets
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Bake the Lasagna
  6. Let it Rest and Serve
